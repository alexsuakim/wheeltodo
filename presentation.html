The Problem

You have a to-do list.
You're still not doing anything.

😩

The Solution

Let the
wheel decide.

Add tasks, give it a spin, start a Pomodoro. On off days, rest activities protect your streak. Simple.

2

3

Focus timer auto-runs

"Start Focus" → Pomodoro starts and tracks your time.

4

Rest days count too

Log rest activities to protect your streak on off days.

Meet Rocky.

Named after the alien in Project Hail Mary — a being that communicates across completely different worlds. Rocky is our AI Slack agent that reads through everything so you never have to.

In progress ionstudio internal

Codebase for non-devs
